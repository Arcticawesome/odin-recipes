Summer Grilled Shrimp Salad

Description

Dish containing Grilled Shrimp and a Cilantro Vinaigrette

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2.25 teaspoons smokehouse maple seasoning
  • 1.5 teaspoons lemon juice
  • 12 ounces peeled and deveined shrimp
Cilantro Vinaigrette:
  • 0.25 cup extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lime juice
  • 2 tablespoons chopped cilantro
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
Salad:
  • 4 cups mixed salad greens, or more to taste
  • 0.5 cup thinly sliced English cucumber
  • 0.33 cup freshly cooked corn
  • 0.5 cup diced tomato
  • 0.25 cup sliced red onion
  • 1 avacado, diced

Steps

    Step 1

  1. Combine olive oil, maple seasoning, and lemon juice in a glass bowl. Add shrimp and toss to coat. Refrigerate until ready to grill.
